Freezeflame Saloon

A Swing Door Saloon Remix

If you have seen the Swing Door Saloon (Surviv.io Saloon Music) page, you may have already seen this one.
This is yet another remix of Swing Door Saloon, but I really love this one so I decided to publish it on Musescore.
And I'm sorry that this is not for pianos.

This remix is inspired by Newer Super Mario Bros. Wii's Freezeflame Glacier and Volcano world map music, as well as 12 Etudes, Op. 9, No. 9 by Rayson Tam on Musescore.
If you can't tell, the name of this remix comes from Freezeflame Glacier/Volcano.
