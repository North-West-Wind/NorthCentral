Chirp

This is one of the music discs in Minecraft. It is composed by C418, and is part of his Volume Beta.
Sadly, Microsoft ruins everything (as always).
C418 does not want Microsoft to own the copyrights to his musics, and Microsoft does not want to use musics they do not own.
As a result, Minecraft no longer has new C418 tracks.

This sheet music created by me is by no mean to be perfect.
The bass clef kind of sucks.

You can always find C418's tracks online.
