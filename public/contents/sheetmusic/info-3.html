NewerSMBW Underwater

(Newer Super Mario Bros. Wii)

Newer Super Mario Bros. Wii (NewerSMBW) is a romhack of New Super Mario Bros. Wii (NSMBW) developed by the Newer Team.
It is an amazing romhack, with custom levels, world maps, power-ups and more! NewerSMBW is still very popular today despite its history of nearly 10 years.
If you want to get into NSMBW romhacks, you should definitely try this one.

The underwater theme is one of the best soundtracks from the romhack.
...I mean all of the soundtracks created are great, but the underwater theme is even better.
It is the most calming and relaxing soundtrack I have ever listened to. (Except Swing Door Saloon)

The sheet music I created attempts to transfer the feeling to piano.
It's kind of like a Waltz.

If you would like to listen to all the soundtracks, you can visit the NewerSMBW page.
