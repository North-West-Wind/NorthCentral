Mom Hid My Game!

Previously known as Hidden My Game By Mom

This sheet music is the main theme of Mom Hid My Game, a game where mom hid your game, and you are going to find it.
The levels are filled with ridiculous and hilarious things, like having a T-junction in the middle of your house.
The music goes along with the funny and happy vibes the game offers.
It is one of the best off-beat fun music I have ever listened to.

So I made a piano sheet out of it.
