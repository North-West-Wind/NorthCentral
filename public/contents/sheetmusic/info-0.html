Swing Door Saloon

A.K.A. Surviv.io Saloon Music

This is made because I couldn't find any Surviv.io saloon piano sheet music on the Internet.
While a lot of people first heard this from Surviv.io's Desert Rain Map, this wonderful piece of music was actually composed by Crispin Merrell.
It is called Swing Door Saloon on Squirky Music.
The elevator music you are listening to right now (if it is not broken and you have turned on your volume) is based of Swing Door Saloon.
